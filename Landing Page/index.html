NTHUCity

Coordinate NTHU System And Build Your Own City


Inspiration

Have you ever logged into the NTHU e-Learning platform and found out there's nothing posted on it? That's because there are so many different platforms for NTHU e-Learning system.

Doesn't that bother you that there's no coordinating e-Learning system and mobile app for you to check out your score and homework? Don't worry! We could help you solve this problem in an interesting way!

Features

System coordination


We provide an app that coordinates all the NTHU
e-Learing platforms(e.g., iLMS,Moodle,eLearn). You can check out all the lectures' information in just one app.

NTHU Map


We also create a NTHU map with all the school buildings on it. You could also find the information from all the departments in that building.

Notification


There's a small tip by the side of every building. You could check out the latest news through it.

Customization


Most interesting of all, you could also build your own buildings with simple steps. Every building is like a bookmark which contains a website access. But you don't have to access an external website linkage, you could read the website information through our app.
